## What Is KeilHQ?

KeilHQ is a unified work platform that replaces your entire productivity stack — task management, team chat, docs, calendar, meeting transcription, and an AI assistant — in a single connected workspace.

Instead of juggling 5–6 disconnected tools (Slack + Asana + Notion + Otter.ai + Google Calendar), teams use KeilHQ as their single source of truth for planning, communicating, documenting, and shipping work.

Everything is connected. A decision made in chat becomes a task. A task links to a doc. A meeting transcript creates action items. Your AI assistant reads all of it and tells you what to focus on next.

---

## Core Features

### The Clarity Engine (Our Differentiator)

Most task tools let you create vague tickets like "Fix the thing." KeilHQ doesn't.

Every task is born with **Objectives** and **Success Criteria** — defined upfront so everyone knows what "done" looks like before work begins. The AI helps extract clear requirements from messy asks, chats, and meeting notes.

Result: no ambiguous tasks, no "what does done look like?" meetings, no rework from misaligned expectations.

### Smart Task Management

- Kanban boards, Gantt timelines, and calendar views
- Task dependencies with automatic blocking logic
- Parent-child task hierarchies for breaking down epics
- Multi-assignee support, threaded comments, full audit trail
- Shareable public task links for client visibility

### Smart Dashboard

Stop wasting 20 minutes every morning figuring out what to do. The dashboard auto-ranks your work into Immediate, Today, Blocked, and Backlog — based on real priority, due dates, and dependency state.

### Motion — Built-in Docs & Knowledge Base

Block-based rich-text editor (Notion-quality) built directly into the workspace:
- Real-time collaborative editing
- Subpage hierarchy for full knowledge bases
- Comment threads on any block
- Public shareable links, lock/unlock pages

### Real-Time Team Chat

Slack-style channels and DMs with instant delivery — minus the separate subscription:
- Group channels, direct messages, unread badges
- Connected to tasks and docs (no more "what was that message about?")

### Google Calendar Integration

Two-way sync between your calendar and tasks. Scheduled tasks appear as calendar events. Changes flow both directions in real time.

### Meeting Recordings & Transcription

Record meetings directly in KeilHQ. Audio is transcribed automatically with speaker diarization — identifying who said what. Turn decisions into tasks in one click.

### AI Assistant (Context-Aware)

Not a generic chatbot. KeilHQ AI reads your actual workspace data:
- "What's blocking my team right now?" — answered with real context
- Creates tasks from conversation
- Auto-schedules unscheduled work into free calendar slots
- Connects GitHub issues/PRs to workspace tasks
- Multi-agent architecture with specialized capabilities for tasks, chat, docs, scheduling, and code

### Integrations

Live: Google Calendar, GitHub, Gmail, Notion (import/export), AWS S3, Sarvam AI transcription, ElevenLabs, multi-model AI (Gemini, OpenRouter, OpenAI, local models).

Coming soon: Linear, Jira, Slack, Google Meet, Google Drive.
