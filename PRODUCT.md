# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Teams, developers, and professionals managing projects, collaborative documents (Motion), tasks, and meeting recordings.

## Product Purpose
A clean, high-performance workspace combining document editing, real-time sync, task tracking, and audio recordings/transcription.

## Brand Personality
* Minimalist
* Clean
* High-productivity

## Anti-references
* Overly rounded cards (radius > 16px)
* Space-wasting layouts
* Loud, non-functional animations
* Generic AI slop aesthetics

## Design Principles
* **Precision Spacing**: Compact, highly structured vertical grid that prioritizes text readability.
* **Typographic Contrast**: Refined hierarchy with clean sans-serif typefaces, precise letter-spacing, and line-heights.
* **Invisible UX**: Zero-latency transitions for frequent keyboard actions, and subtle spring-based scale feedback on mouse interactions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
* High-contrast text compliance (WCAG AA).
* Safe touch-target bounds (>=44pt hit areas) for mobile interactives.
* Respect for prefers-reduced-motion settings.
